Ramen sandwich

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Ramen Packet
  • Sliced Cheese
  • Lunch meat
  • Egg

    • Assembly

    Boil water in a pot

    Hold ramen with tongs, dip in boiling water for exactly 10 seconds

    Crack egg in heated pan, smash ramen into egg and let it cook

    Add ham, cheese and a little of the ramen flavor packet on top of cooking ramen

    Set tempreture of pan to low and let cook

    Butter bread and sit the ramen in between the slices

    Crisp both sides of bread
