Burrio Recipe

Ingredients

  • one tortilla
  • 1lb of ground beef
  • taco seasoning mix
  • lettuce
  • refried beans
  • hot sauce
  • 1 yellow onion
  • shredded cheese
  • sour cream

    • Assembly

    Prepare ground beef by heating it in a pan until brown.

    Chop up half a yellow onion.

    Add onion and taco seasoning mix into pan alongside ground beef, simmer until fully cooked to taste.

    Heat refried beans in a microwave dish.

    Chop up lettuce on a cutting board.

    Start with tortilla on a plate.

    Add ground beef, beans and cheese.

    Add lettuce. Add sour cream. Add hot sauce.

    Distrubute items evenly so bites aren't lopsided.

    Wrap tortilla.
